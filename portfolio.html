MEDIEVAL ADVENTURE GAMETURNBASED SUPERHERO GAME

WORLD TUNER

A third person platformer made in a week long game jam in wich the theme was, in portuguese "Ambiente". "Ambiente" can be interpreted as either ambient or enviorment. My team (me and another dude) decided to read ir as ambient and made a game in wich the main character can change the ambient music and controll the world. Different musics would have different effects in game and could in theory be used to solve multiple puzzles. In the end we were able to implement 3 of them and made built a small map in order to showcase the possible future game. Eventuallywe plan on finishing it. ALSO MELHORAR ISTO
